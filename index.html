Chorpleth Mapping

This choropleth map showcases the concentrations of Black and African American populations within the counties of Minnesota circa 2020. This map allows us to see that in counties near the Twin Cities and along the Great Lake border, the total population of the counties has a higher percentage of Black and African Americans.

Bivariate Choropleth Mapping

This dataset looks at the percentage per county of rural populations and Black and African American populations, respectively, to identify patterns in residential locations. This dataset was obtained from the 2020 Census. The intended color pattern was orange and purple, but feel free to view the different color schemes available!

Proportional Symbol Mapping

This is a proportional symbol map that shows the locations of retired privately owned wells within the state of Iowa as of 2020. The data was obtained from the Iowa Department of Natural Resources. Overall, there is a clear concentration of retired wells along the western, northern, and eastern protion of the state.

Flow Mapping

Clicking on the image or title above will open a more indepth profile about this flow map and the in-nation migratory patterns that occurred from 1887-1924 in the United States.

Isarithmic Mapping

This isarithmic map produced by my professor, Caglar Koylu, showcases yearly total precipitation within the state of Iowa. This dataset was obtained from The Iowa Environmental Mesonet (IEM), and shows that the northeastern portion of Iowan counties recieves the highest percent of annual precipitation.

Final Project

This map is part of a larger visualization collection that highlights the economic and environmental health results of the rocket activity on Cape Canaveral Island in Brevard County, Florida. This research project was done in collaboration with my fellow student, Nina Osborne.
